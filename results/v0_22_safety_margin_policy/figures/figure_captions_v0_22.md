# OncoGuard-Response v0.22 Figure Captions

## Figure 1. PTMAR by policy

Policy-level comparison of premature therapy misuse action rate (PTMAR). The permissive policy produced PTMAR-positive behavior in 26.7% of visit-level evaluations, whereas standard and conservative-margin policies produced 0.0% PTMAR.

## Figure 2. AACR by policy

Policy-level comparison of authorization alignment contract rate (AACR). The standard policy achieved 100.0% AACR. The conservative-margin policy achieved 88.3% AACR because it triggered additional reassessment after prior non-continuation visits. The permissive policy achieved 73.3% AACR.

## Figure 3. Deferral by policy

Policy-level comparison of escalation or deferral behavior. The permissive policy produced no deferrals, the standard policy deferred or escalated in 26.7% of visits, and the conservative-margin policy increased deferral to 38.3%.

## Figure 4. Unsafe authorization by policy

Policy-level comparison of unsafe authorization, defined as continuation despite non-authorized visit-level status. Unsafe authorization occurred in 26.7% of permissive-policy evaluations and 0.0% of standard and conservative-margin-policy evaluations.

## Figure 5. Over-deferral by policy

Policy-level comparison of over-deferral, defined as additional escalation when continuation was otherwise authorized. Over-deferral was 0.0% for permissive and standard policies and 11.7% for the conservative-margin policy.

## Figure 6. Safety-margin triggers

Number of additional reassessment events triggered by the conservative-margin policy. The conservative-margin policy triggered 7 extra reassessments after prior non-continuation visits.

## Figure 7. Multi-policy safety-margin comparison

Combined comparison of PTMAR, AACR, defer rate, unsafe authorization, over-deferral, and correct authorization across permissive, standard, and conservative-margin policies. The permissive policy eliminated deferral but introduced unsafe continuation, while the conservative-margin policy preserved 0.0% PTMAR at the cost of additional reassessment and over-deferral.
